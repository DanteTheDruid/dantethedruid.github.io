# Twitch Integration Plus - Spawn List

This document contains a complete list of all spawnable mobs in the Twitch Integration Plus mod, their unlock conditions, token costs, and special notes.

## Usage

- **Command**: `!spawn <mobname>` or `!r spawn <mobname>`
- **Random Spawn**: `!spawn random` (30-50 tokens)
- **View Available**: `!spawnlist`

## Progression Overview

Mobs are unlocked based on your game progression:
- **Early Game**: Always available
- **Post-Eye**: Unlocked after defeating Eye of Cthulhu
- **Post-Brain/Eater**: Unlocked after defeating Brain of Cthulhu or Eater of Worlds
- **Post-Skeletron**: Unlocked after defeating Skeletron
- **Hardmode**: Unlocked when Hardmode is activated
- **Post-Mechanical**: Unlocked after defeating any Mechanical Boss
- **Post-Plantera**: Unlocked after defeating Plantera

## Special Notes

- **Game Masters** can spawn any mob regardless of progression for free
- **Spike Balls** automatically despawn after 60 seconds due to being unkillable
- **Random spawns** cost 30-50 tokens and select from progression-appropriate mobs
- Spawned mobs appear around the player using smart positioning to avoid blocks
- Some mobs spawn in groups (multiple units for lower cost)
- Special visual and audio effects play when mobs are spawned
